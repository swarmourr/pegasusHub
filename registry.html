Goal of the Registry

Community-Driven Blueprints

The Scientific Registry is a centralized repository of production-ready workflows contributed by researchers around the world. Instead of starting from scratch, you can search for a blueprint that matches your domain—whether it's machine learning, bioinformatics, or astrophysics—and adapt it to fit your own computational needs.
